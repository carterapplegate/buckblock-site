What it does

Focus you can feel in your hand

BuckBlock pairs an iOS app with a physical NFC tag — the "BuckBlock Station." No willpower battles with your settings screen. Just tap and go.

NFC

Tap to lock

Hold your phone to the BuckBlock Station. Pick a duration — 15 minutes to indefinite — and your distracting apps are shielded by Apple's Screen Time.

✓

Always-blocked essentials

Social media and games are locked down by default. Music, messages, and utility apps stay toggleable so you can still get class notes through.

◎

Tap again to unlock

Indefinite sessions end the moment you tap the tag again. Timed sessions release themselves. No early-unlock loopholes hiding in a menu.

How it works

Four steps from distracted to done

STEP 01
📲

Tap the NFC chip

Hold your phone to the BuckBlock Station on your desk, backpack, or dorm wall.

STEP 02
⏱️

Pick a duration

Choose 15m, 30m, an hour, two hours, a custom time — or indefinite.

STEP 03
🔒

Apps get blocked

Screen Time shields your selected apps. Notifications for those apps go quiet too.

STEP 04
✅

Scan again to unlock

Timer ends on its own, or tap the tag a second time to break an indefinite session.
